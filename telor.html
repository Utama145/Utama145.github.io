What is UNAI?


UNAI Campus by UNAI Multimedia 2022.

UNAI is Higher Education institution managed by the Seventh-day Adventist Church of the Western Union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that it was founded in cimindi in 1929 which was orginally called Opleiding School der Advent Zending. in the past , this school was founded for the education of the evangelists of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.

Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 faculties:

1) Faculty of Philosophy
2) Faculty of Theacher Training & Education
3) Faculty of Economic
4) Faculty of Nursing
5) Faculty of Mathematies and Natural Sciences
6) Faculty of Informatiion Technology

The chemical formula for water is H2O, and Albert Einstein's energy formula is E = mc2.
